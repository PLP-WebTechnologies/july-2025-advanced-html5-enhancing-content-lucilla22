An Overview of Basic Statistics

Key Statistical Concepts

Statistics is the science of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data. Here are a few fundamental concepts we use daily.

  • Mean (Average)
  • Median (Middle Value)
  • Mode (Most Frequent Value)
  • Standard Deviation
